SOFTWARE AS A SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Software as a Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 15, 2025
("Effective Date") by and between CloudTech Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation
("Provider") and Global Enterprises Ltd., a UK company ("Customer").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Provider offers cloud-based software services; and
WHEREAS, Customer desires to subscribe to such services;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties
agree as follows:

1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Service" means the cloud-based software platform provided by Provider, including
all updates, enhancements, and related documentation.

1.2 "Customer Data" means all data, content, and materials uploaded, stored, or
processed by Customer using the Service.

1.3 "Authorized Users" means Customer's employees, contractors, and agents authorized
to access the Service.

2. SERVICE LEVELS AND AVAILABILITY

2.1 Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain the Service.

2.2 The Service shall be available 99.5% of the time during each calendar month,
calculated on a monthly basis. Scheduled maintenance windows are excluded from
availability calculations.

2.3 In the event of service interruptions, Provider will use reasonable efforts to
restore service as promptly as possible. No service credits, refunds, or other
compensation will be provided for downtime or service degradation.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Customer shall pay Provider annual subscription fees of $120,000 USD per year,
payable in advance.

3.2 All invoices shall be paid within sixty (60) days of invoice date. Late payments
shall incur interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by
law, whichever is lower.

3.3 Provider reserves the right to increase fees upon renewal by up to 15% annually
or CPI plus 5%, whichever is greater, with 30 days' notice to Customer.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 Initial Term: This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue
for an initial term of four (4) years.

4.2 Automatic Renewal: This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive
one-year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least
thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

4.3 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon
written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure
such breach within ten (10) days of receiving written notice.

4.4 Effect of Termination: Upon termination, Customer's access to the Service will
cease immediately, and Provider may delete all Customer Data after thirty (30) days.

5. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

5.1 Provider will process Customer Data in accordance with applicable laws, including
GDPR where applicable.

5.2 The parties agree to execute a Data Processing Addendum if required by applicable
law. Provider will use Customer Data solely to provide the Service and will not use it
for any other purpose without Customer's prior written consent.

5.3 Provider processes data in multiple global data centers, including facilities
located in the United States, Singapore, and Ireland, based on capacity and performance
optimization.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

6.1 Provider owns all right, title, and interest in the Service, including all
intellectual property rights therein.

6.2 Customer retains ownership of Customer Data. Customer grants Provider a limited
license to use Customer Data solely to provide the Service.

6.3 Any custom developments, modifications, or enhancements to the Service created
for Customer shall be owned by Provider, with Customer receiving a non-exclusive,
non-transferable license to use such developments as part of the Service.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY INDEMNITY

7.1 Provider will defend Customer against any third-party claims alleging that the
Service infringes such third party's intellectual property rights, provided that
Customer promptly notifies Provider in writing of such claim and cooperates with
Provider's defense.

7.2 Provider's obligations under this Section are conditioned upon Customer granting

7.3 This Section states Provider's entire liability and Customer's exclusive remedy
for intellectual property infringement claims.
